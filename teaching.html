Teaching

Information Visualization

I spent five semesters as a teaching assistant for NYU Tandon's Information Visualization course. I have developed collections of Observable notebooks to help teach students how to build visualizations with D3. The notebooks contain tutorials and examples, practice exercises, and homework assignments.

Visualization for Machine Learning

I spent one semester as a teaching assistant for Northeastern's Visualization for Machine Learning course. I created a collection of Jupyter notebooks for teaching machine learning explainability concepts using Altair.

Online D3 Help

Outside of class, I enjoy helping people learn D3 online. I've answered over 75 questions about D3 on Stack Overflow and the Observable forums.
