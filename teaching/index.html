Teaching

Information Visualization

I have spent five semesters as a TA for NYU Tandon's Information Visualization course. I have developed collections of Observable notebooks to help teach students how to build visualizations with D3. The notebooks contain tutorials and examples, practice exercises, and homeworks.

Online D3 Help

Outside of class, I enjoy helping people learn D3 online. I've answered over 50 questions about D3 on Stack Overflow and the Observable forums.
